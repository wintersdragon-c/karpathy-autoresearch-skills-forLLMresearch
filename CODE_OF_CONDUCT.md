# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

Be respectful, direct, and technically honest.

Examples of expected behavior:

- discuss design and test evidence concretely
- challenge ideas without attacking people
- report uncertainty instead of bluffing
- keep reviews focused on bugs, regressions, and contract drift

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- harassment or personal attacks
- dismissive or hostile review comments
- knowingly misleading claims about test results or repository state
- publishing private or security-sensitive information without consent

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove comments, issues, or contributions that violate these standards and may restrict further participation when necessary.
